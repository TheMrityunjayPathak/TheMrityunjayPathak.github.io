GitHub Application Swagger UI

‚ä≥ In the used car market, buyers and sellers often struggle to determine a fair price for their vehicles.

‚ä≥ This project aims to provide an accurate and transparent pricing for used cars by analyzing real-world data.

Dashly : Live Sales Dashboard

GitHub Dashbaord

Problem

‚ä≥ Quick Buy is a leading superstore operating across the United States.

‚ä≥ It manages thousands of product transactions daily across multiple regions.

‚ä≥ The store's operations relied on manual spreadsheets and SQL queries to track business performance.

‚ä≥ As a result, decision-making was slowed down and made it harder to identify growth opportunities.


Solution

‚ä≥ Designed a fully automated ETL pipeline using Python, SQLAlchemy and GitHub Actions for seamless daily data updates.

‚ä≥ Built custom Python ETL scripts to extract, transform and load over 50,000+ sales records into a Neon PostgreSQL cloud database.

‚ä≥ Automated daily data generation (~100 new transactions daily) to simulate real-time sales activity and maintain a continuously refreshed dataset.

‚ä≥ Integrated Power BI directly with the database, enabling real-time auto-refreshing dashboard without manual uploads.


Key Insights

‚ä≥ Standard Class drives ~60% of total sales (~‚Çπ5.1M) and profit (~‚Çπ897K), making it the most profitable and preferred shipping mode.

‚ä≥ Consumer Segment generates ~50% of total revenue (~‚Çπ4.26M) and profit (~‚Çπ757K), highlighting it as the primary customer base.

‚ä≥ Q4 (Oct-Dec) delivers ~27% of yearly revenue, suggesting a strong seasonal demand, ideal for marketing and inventory planning.

‚ä≥ Paper, Binders and Phones emerge as top-performing sub-categories, together making up ~45% of total revenue.

‚ä≥ West and East regions lead the market with ~58% of total sales, while the South region with ~19% shows room for growth.

‚ä≥ Top 5 States (CA, NY, TX, PA, OH) contribute ~54% of total sales, with CA alone driving ~21%, showing strong regional concentration.
